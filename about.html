Open Arms Foundation

The stablisment of this foundation is to help people around the world that because of economic status they are unable to continue their study. Our main goal is to make their dreams come true.

Open Arms Foundation has made significant progress in its mission to provide high-quality education to all students. The concept was to provide world-class education to deserving students from various backgrounds, as well as to cultivate and nurture their minds. Over the years, and the foundation has received international recognition for its excellence in school education.
